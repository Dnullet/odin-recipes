Roman Style Lemon Ice

Description

A classic italian ice dessert from the Talisman Italian Cook Book. Enjoy this during the summertime. Serves 6.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 quart water
  • 3/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 egg white
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 jiggers rum

Steps

  1. Combine sugar and water in saucepan and heat until sugar melts
  2. Add lemon juice and strain through very fine sieve
  3. Freeze until mushy, stirring frequently, and remove from freezer.
  4. Add stiffly beaten egg white and 1/4 cup sugar and mix well. Add rum and mix.
  5. Return to freezer and continue freezing to ice consistency, stirring very often.
